Course Description
This is a calculus based introduction to physics and is part of a two-semester physics sequence that covers mechanics and electrodynamics. Calculus I is a prerequisite and Calculus II is a corequisite. The lecture is three credits while the lab is one credit. We will spend approximately 2-4 classes on each topic (see below) and do a corresponding lab. The exact pace of the course will depend on the overall skill level of the class and is at the discretion of the instructor.
